  1. STEP 01

    Install Later.

    Or don't. We'll still be here next Tuesday. Statistically, so will your tasks.

    Later recognizes the shape of a task you'll never do. It knows the ones. It has friends.

  3. STEP 03

    Close the app.

    Later handles the rest. Usually by not handling it. That's the feature, not a bug.
